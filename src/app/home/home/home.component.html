TMSB has been recognised by various industry experts and music panels as bringing in state of the art infrastructure, curriculum to mould novices, to expert musicians. Guitar

We provide individualized instruction in both electric and acoustic guitar, allowing students to select either of these as their principle instrument. The course work for each is specifically designed and customized for all levels of playing ability.

Vocals

Singing has always been the centrepiece of music, for centuries. The voice is a powerful tool, which effectively explores the world of music, and knows no bounds. Here, at TMSB, the voice is trained and disciplined to exercise control and power, enabling students to test their limits and go beyond what they thought was possible.

Keyboard

A direct descendant of the piano, the keyboard is a similar and less expensive option, for many students who wish to gain a basic understanding of musical notes and piano playing. It is popular due to its portability and multifunctional features, making it easily accessible for anyone an everyone to put in their homes or rooms.

Drum

At TMSB, the students undergo structured learning under the supervision of highly experienced drum faculty. The Drum program also stresses on the importance of students gaining first-hand access to some of the best equipment and techniques available.
